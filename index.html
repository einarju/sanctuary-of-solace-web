Crystal Healing

Natural energy healing with crystals placed on the body

What it is

Crystal healing is a natural energy healing therapy channelled through the practitioner and enhanced through crystals placed on various parts of the body. It gently balances life energies and supports the physical, emotional and spiritual bodies.

What to expect

Sacred space is opened before you arrive. You’ll complete a new client questionnaire (or a session sheet for returning clients), then lie on a massage table/plinth fully clothed. The practitioner scans the energy field, then places crystals as guided, followed by aura clearing and grounding.

How often

Recommended: a minimum of six consecutive weekly sessions to work through an issue (each situation is different; it’s always your choice).

Sound Healing Therapy coming soon

Vibration-based relaxation and nervous system support

Important Notice

You cannot receive Sound Healing Therapy if:

  • You are currently receiving any form of Cancer Treatment.
  • Are within trimester one of pregnancy.

Also:

If you have Trigeminal neuralgia Sound Healing Therapy cannot be given around the head area of the body.

If you are being treated for a serious physical or mental disorder or are taking medications; before you make an appointment, you are required to provide a letter signed and dated by your Medical Doctor stating that they have no objections to you receiving Sound Healing Treatment. This original letter must be provided to the Sound Healing Practitioner prior to any Sound Healing Treatment being provided.

What to expect

You’ll complete a questionnaire and discuss what you’d like support with. You’ll lie fully clothed on the table (or receive treatment in a chair/wheelchair if needed). Bowls may be placed on different parts of the body (face down and face up), and other instruments may be used. The session ends with review and grounding.

How often

Recommended: at least six consecutive weekly sessions per issue (your choice).

Cancellation Policy, Appointments and Pricing

There is considerable preparation and time spent by Sanctuary of Solace and/or its practitioners in preparing for each client in advance. Sanctuary of Solace is committed to providing professional personalised services and care to all clients. Client's time is precious and respected and the same is expected in return.

You are advised to read the modality service you require in the relevant modality section on this website prior to booking an appointment. Please note, booking an appointment is not a confirmation of your appointment. Your appointment is not confirmed until you receive a confirmation in writing.

Appointments

Please note a booking of an appointment is NOT a confirmation of your appointment. Sanctuary of Solace will be in contact with you to confirm the closest appointment time available to suite your request.

Booking your appointment

If you are a new client, it is recommended that when booking your initial appointment, you also book in your follow up appointment/s due to potential waiting times. New clients are requested to arrive at least 05 minutes prior to your appointment time to complete the New Client Consultation Form. Once an appointment is made, Sanctuary of Solace will contact you directly to clarify and confirm the modality you wish to receive.

Courtesy Reminders

When making an appointment with Sanctuary of Solace you will receive a courtesy reminder three days prior your appointment.

Time keeping - Arriving for your scheduled appointment

Whether your scheduled appointment is in person, on-line or distant, please arrive 05 minutes in advance of your scheduled appointment. Out of respect of other clients and the Practitioner, late arrivals will result in you not benefiting from the full session and your scheduled appointment will be cut short so that the next clients appointment starts on time and you will be charged for the full amount.

Cancellation Policy and Fees

Cancellation fees will be applied where:

  • A 'no show' or cancellation less than 24 hours from the time of the scheduled appointment will result in a cancellation fee of 25% of the initial fee being added to any future session.
  • Cancellation on the day of the booked appointment will incur a cancellation fee of the full amount (the day being noted as commencing at 00.01 of the appointment day).
  • Cancellation fees will be required to be paid within 48 hours, prior to any future appointment and the appointment fee paid in person immediately following the session.
  • If the potential client has paid the fee in full in advance of the booked appointment, Sanctuary of Solace reserves the right to retain the full fee depending on the scenarios above.
  • Sanctuary of Solace reserves the right to cancel appointments at short notice in the event of any unforeseen circumstances. If such an event occurs, no cancellation fee as above will apply.

If you are unwell to attend for your booked appointment your appointment can be easily changed to an on-line session or distance healing where relevant, which works equally well as in person depending on the therapy booked. This is an effective method to avoid a cancellation fee and you will also receive the required support. Please note, only Reiki and Emotional and Metaphysical Kinesiology can be provided on-line. Reiki can also be provided through distance healing.

The above applies to all modalities and appointments, including initial consultation bookings.

Payment and Payment Methods

Non cash payments

Non cash payments must be made when booking an appointment, are not refundable or transferable. Non cash payments are accepted by Revolut, Electronic Funds Transfer to a nominated bank account and PayPal. Please note, if making payment by any of these methods please contact Sanctuary of Solace immediately by email after you make the appointment for details.

Please note that a bank/credit/debit card surcharge fee may be incurred with all visa and Mastercard payments.

On-line or distant healing sessions must be paid and received in full in advance of any session.

Cash payments

For cash payments, full payment is due at the end of each session. At present, there is no requirement to part pay or pay in full in when making an appointment if payment is a cash payment.

In person appointments

In person appointments must be paid on the day, immediately following the session with any previous cancellation fees paid within 48 hours of the scheduled appointment.

On-line or distant appointments

On-line or distant healing sessions must be paid and received in full 48 hours in advance of any session. Please note, only Reiki and Emotional and Metaphysical Kinesiology can be provided on-line. Reiki can also be provided through distance healing.
